ZAP!
“ZAP!” is an interactive, biomimetic installation inspired by the electric eel’s electrocommunication. The installation translates eel’s ability to sense and interact with its environment through electromagnetic fields into a large-scale, multi-sensory experience. This interdisciplinary projectcombines sound art, architectural sculpture, and interactive design to create an immersive environment. The installation comprises three integrated layers: a physical sculpture embodying the eel’s anatomy, a live-generated soundscape reflecting its behavior, and interactive elements that allow spectators to engage with the simulated ecosystem.
